# AbleLink Community & Safety Policies

## Code of Conduct
AbleLink is a safe space. We have ZERO TOLERANCE for:
- Hate speech or discrimination based on disability, race, gender, etc.
- Harassment or bullying.
- Predatory behavior or financial scams.

## AI Moderation
All public posts and messages are scanned by our AI Safety System.
- If your content is flagged, it will be hidden immediately.
- Repeated violations result in a permanent ban.
- False positives can be appealed by contacting support@ablelink.com.

## SOS Feature
In case of emergency:
1. Press the RED "SOS" button (available on all pages for disabled users).
2. Your location is sent to your Emergency Contacts.
3. Local emergency services are NOT automatically called; this alerts your trusted circle.
