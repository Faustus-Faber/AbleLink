# Accessibility Features Help

## Visual Customization
You can adjust the interface to your needs:
- **Font Size**: Small, Normal, Large, Extra Large.
- **Contrast**: Normal, High Contrast (Yellow on Black), Inverted.
- **Color Blindness**: Filters for Protanopia, Deuteranopia, Tritanopia.
- **Animation**: "Reduce Motion" stops all non-essential animations.

## Voice Navigation
(Desktop Only)
- Say "Scroll down" to move the page.
- Say "Go to [Link Name]" to navigate.
- Say "Read this" to hear text under your mouse.

## Document OCR
Upload any image (JPG, PNG) or Scanned PDF to the "OCR & Simplify" tool.
- The system extracts text using Optical Character Recognition.
- You can choosing "Simplify" to have AI rewrite complex sentences into plain language.
